How It Works

Up and running in 60 seconds

No setup, no configuration, no account. Just install and start scheduling.

1
Install TabTimer
Add TabTimer to Chrome from the Chrome Web Store. It's free and takes under 10 seconds.
2
Right-Click Any Page
Right-click any webpage and choose "TabTimer → Schedule this page" or press Alt+L to open the scheduler instantly.
3
Set Your Schedule
Pick your date, time, how often it repeats, and whether to auto-close after opening. Click Schedule.
4
Sit Back & Relax
TabTimer handles everything from here. Your pages open automatically, right on time, every time.
