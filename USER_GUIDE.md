## Getting Started

### Installation

1. **Download** the TabTimer ZIP file
2. **Extract** the ZIP to a folder on your computer
3. Open Chrome and navigate to `chrome://extensions`
4. Enable **Developer mode** (toggle in the top right corner)
5. Click **"Load unpacked"**
6. Select the folder containing `manifest.json`
7. TabTimer is now installed! You'll see the clock icon in your toolbar.

### First Launch

When you first install TabTimer:

## Creating Your First Schedule

### Method 1: From Any Webpage (Fastest)

1. Navigate to the website you want to schedule
2. Press **Alt+L** on your keyboard
3. A popup appears - set your date and time
4. Click **"Schedule"**
5. Done! The page will open automatically at your scheduled time.

### Method 2: Right-Click Menu

1. Right-click anywhere on the webpage
2. Select **"TabTimer â†’ Schedule this page"**
3. Configure your schedule in the popup
4. Click **"Schedule"**

### Method 3: From the Management Page

1. Click the TabTimer icon in your toolbar
2. Click **"Manage Schedules"** (or press Alt+O)
3. Click **"+ Add New"** in the sidebar
4. Fill in the details:
   - **URL**: The website address
   - **Name**: A friendly name (optional but recommended)
   - **Category**: Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Once, or Other
   - **Open at**: Date and time to open
   - **Repeat Schedule**: How often to repeat
   - **Auto re-lock**: How long before it re-locks for the next open
5. Click **"Create Schedule"**

## Repeat Options Explained

### No Repeat (One-time)
Opens once at the scheduled time, then marks as complete.

### Daily
Opens every day at the same time.

### Weekdays (Mon-Fri)
Opens only on weekdays, skipping weekends.

### Weekends (Sat-Sun)
Opens only on Saturday and Sunday.

### Weekly
Opens once per week on the same day.

### Every 2 Weeks / Every 3 Weeks
Opens every 2 or 3 weeks on the same day.

### Monthly
Opens once per month on the same date.

### Every 2 Months / Quarterly / Every 6 Months
Opens at longer monthly intervals.

### Yearly
Opens once per year on the same date.

### Leap Year (Every 4 Years)
Opens every 4 years - great for leap year events!

### Every X Minutes (Premium)
Opens repeatedly at minute intervals. Perfect for:
- Entering the same sweepstakes multiple times
- Refreshing a page regularly
- Example: Set to 10 minutes, opens at 2:00, 2:10, 2:20, etc.

### Every X Hours (Premium)
Opens at hourly intervals throughout the day.

## Tips & Tricks

### Auto-Delete by Date

Name your schedule with a date prefix to auto-delete:
- `2026-01-31 Amazon Gift Card Giveaway`
- `2026-02-14 Valentine's Contest`

After that date passes, the schedule is automatically removed!

### Grace Period for Missed Schedules

If your computer was off when a schedule was supposed to open:
- TabTimer will open it within **2 minutes** of turning on
- Never miss a scheduled opening!

### Best Practices for Sweepstakes

1. **Use auto-stagger**: When importing multiple URLs, stagger by 30-60 seconds to avoid overwhelming your browser
2. **Set auto re-lock**: Use 5-10 minutes so recurring schedules reset properly
3. **Use date prefixes**: Name schedules with end dates for automatic cleanup
4. **Organize by category**: Use Daily, Weekly, Monthly to stay organized

### Organizing Large Numbers of Schedules

1. **Search**: Use the search box to find specific schedules
2. **Categories**: Assign categories for filtering
3. **Colors** (Premium): Color-code by priority or type
4. **Date names**: Include dates in names for sorting

## Troubleshooting

### Schedule didn't open

1. **Was Chrome running?** TabTimer needs Chrome open to work
2. **Check the time**: Make sure the scheduled time has passed
3. **Grace period**: If Chrome was closed, wait up to 2 minutes after opening

### "Manifest file is missing" error

1. Make sure you extracted the ZIP file completely
2. The folder should contain `manifest.json` directly (not in a subfolder)
3. Try re-extracting the ZIP

### Schedule opened but page didn't load

1. Check your internet connection
2. Verify the URL is correct
3. Some sites may block automated opens

### Premium features locked after trial

This is expected! Your trial has ended. Options:
- Continue using free features (your schedules are safe!)
- Purchase Premium for $10 lifetime access

### How to reset trial (for testing only)

1. Go to `chrome://extensions`
2. Find TabTimer and click "Remove"
3. Reinstall the extension
4. Note: This also removes all your schedules!
